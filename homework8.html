Blinking Eye

Puppet Animation

Reflection

When creating my blinking eye I didnt go for the realistic look I wanted it to look more like old cartoon style. Instead of showing the upper eyelid closing. I decided to get rid of it to show the blinking effect and then adding it back as if the eye was opening again. I decided to use Naruto for my puppet animation. He is my favorite anime character. I made him move side to side as if he was going to use the rasengan(blue energy ball) in his left hand.
