Reflection

When creating my blinking eye I didnt go for the realistic look I wanted it to look more like old cartoon style. Instead of showing the upper eyelid closing. I decided to get rid of it to show the blinking effect and then adding it back as if the eye was opening again. I decided to use a recent image of my little brother's first Halloween. He was dressed as Buzzlight year. I used this image to move him side to side to make him look as if he was flying.
