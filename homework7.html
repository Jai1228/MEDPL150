Reflection

I started with a photo of a busy city street and changed it into a more graphic, colorful version. I used only three colors — green (#4CAF50), orange (#FF9800), and violet (#9C27B0) — to give the image a bold and creative look. I kept the people in their natural colors so the scene still felt real, but I changed the buildings, street, and sky to flat, solid colors to make it look like an illustration. Then, I added a geometric shape in the sky to make the image more interesting and to show how shapes can change the balance of a design.
